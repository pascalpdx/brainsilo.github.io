Supporting PASCAL


Supporting PASCAL is easy.

Join PASCAL as a member, and get 24⁄7 access to the space, the ability to host your own events, and good feelings. You can find the member application here.

You can also make a individual donations of either money or stuff.

If your company would like to support us with assets, a one time donation, or a recurring pledge, send us an email at donations@pascalpdx.org.

To volunteer, send us an email at volunteer@pascalpdx.org.
