support

Supporting PASCAL is easy. If you or your company would like to support us with assets, a one time donation, or a recurring pledge, send us an email at donations@pascalpdx.org.

events

PASCAL currently holds an Open Day on the Second Saturday of each month.

Come visit us for tours of the space, talks, and fun information security exhibits.

PASCAL’s other upcoming events can be found in the on Calagator and Meetup.

For hosting events, please contact us at events@pascalpdx.org.

about

PASCAL is the Portland Area Scientific and Cultural Advancement League, a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization founded to serve the information security community, educate the interested and the under-served, and to provide a venue for related gatherings, events, and projects.

PASCAL’s venue, a hackerspace and community event space, is located less than one mile NW from the Moda Center, directly across Mississippi Avenue from the Widmer Brewery.
